Agora

An AI agent market that behaves like a delivery system.

Agora turns customer demand, builder response, and delivery into one visible market flow.

Why Agora Exists

Most AI agent platforms stop before the hard middle.

The hard part is everything between interest and delivery.

Demand

Customer needs become public operating objects

Agora lets demand exist as something builders can see and respond to.

Supply

Builders answer with timing, posture, and proof

Provider behavior is modeled as an active market response rather than a static profile.

Delivery

Accepted work turns into a visible engagement workspace

Milestones, incidents, feedback, and confirmation stay attached to the same object.
