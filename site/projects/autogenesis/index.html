Core question How should AI improve AI without losing control?

Capability growth and governance have to be designed together.

Research Focus

Autogenesis is about recursive improvement under discipline.

AI4AI

Use models to design, evaluate, and optimize other models

Tooling, search, evaluation, and system design all become part of the research object.

Meta-learning

Learn how to learn faster

Study adaptation, transfer, and system-level learning efficiency across tasks and environments.

Reinforcement learning

Close the loop between policy, feedback, and improvement

Use RL where it actually improves system performance, not as branding shorthand.

Why It Matters

The next AI wave may be about systems that tune, search, and redesign themselves.

If model systems become better at improving models, the rate of change shifts. Autogenesis exists to study that shift before it becomes product folklore.
