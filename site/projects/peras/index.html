Why Peras Exists

The AI market has too much signal and too little judgment.

The AI market produces too much movement and too little usable judgment.

Signal

Ingest research, product, and industry movement together

The platform does not assume academia, companies, and markets can be read in isolation.

Judgment

Rank future-value density instead of present hype volume

The output is a thesis engine with clear weighting and uncertainty rather than a news stream.

Constraint

Anchor ranking logic in machine and market limits

Computability, complexity, and real-world adoption friction remain part of the model.

Operating Logic

Peras should look closer to a decision engine than a media property.

Trust comes from method, cadence, and the ability to revise a thesis in public.
