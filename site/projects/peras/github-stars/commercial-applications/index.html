Why it matters

Commercial pull often shows up here before market reports catch up

These repos expose what builders and operators actually want to install, run, and test.

Caution

Distribution and novelty can inflate this lane quickly

Peras should pair this category with retention, workflow fit, and enterprise evidence before ranking too aggressively.
