Method

Use stars as one lane, not the whole score

Peras should keep GitHub stars inside a broader ranking stack that also includes product pull, macro bottlenecks, and execution durability.

Why split categories

A research repo and a product repo should not be judged identically

This split reduces category confusion and makes the signal more useful for strategic comparison.

What next

Connect stars back to thesis cards and ranked directions

The long-term goal is not a star dashboard, but a category-aware frontier judgment engine.
