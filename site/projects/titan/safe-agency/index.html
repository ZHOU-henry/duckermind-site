Safe Agency

Agency has to scale with visibility, override, and bounded action.

Titan treats safe agency as the problem of making embodied systems act meaningfully without becoming opaque, brittle, or operationally ungovernable.

Back to TitanSafety Architecture
AgencyVisibility and intervention are part of capability design.

More agentic systems need better supervision interfaces, not only better policy heads.

Bounded action

Do not let policies write their own operating envelope

Action spaces, permissions, and fallback behaviors should be architected, not improvised.

Intervention

Keep operators in the loop when risk rises

Embodied systems need human or policy escalation paths tied to uncertainty and anomaly signals.

Audit

Make failure explainable enough to improve

Without traceability, safe agency becomes post-hoc storytelling rather than a real engineering discipline.
