Noesis Formula

Observatory Index

The observatory index is a first composite frame for regional six-factor comparison, not a final causal model.

Back to Noesis Research Agenda
Interpretation

Weighted factor score minus bottlenecks

The core logic is simple: capability potential rises with factors and falls with hard constraints.

Use

Regional comparison and time slices

The formula supports the dashboard prototype and later historical tracking.

Constraint

Not every factor should be equally weighted

Weights should change by domain and by the maturity of the underlying data.
