Noesis

A disciplined observatory for the AGI symbiosis era.

Noesis studies how humans, machines, and institutions may co-evolve in the AGI era.

Open GitHub Back To Programs
Stage Stage 1 research framework

Charter, source atlas, subprogram docs, and sample briefs are already in place.

Primary domain noesis.duckermind.com

Currently routes into the dossier, later upgradeable to a dedicated research portal.

Core question How should human and machine agency co-evolve?

Institutional adaptation is treated as a design problem, not an afterthought.

Research Scope

Noesis is broader than one philosophical niche.

AGI is not only a model problem. It is also an institutional, cognitive, and economic one.

Umbrella layer

Human-machine symbiosis and institutional redesign

The central question is how humans, machines, and institutions should co-adapt.

Subproject A

Artificial consciousness and machine subjectivity

Investigates whether machine minds can meaningfully become subjects rather than tools.

Subproject B + C

Computation limits and AI economy substrate

Connects machine bounds to compute, data, storage, algorithms, energy, and talent.

Subproject D

Communicative rationality and a new economic model

Builds from Habermas toward post-price coordination, legitimacy, and AI-era economic design.

Methodological Posture

Separate evidence, inference, design, and speculation cleanly.

Noesis keeps evidence, inference, design, and speculation in separate lanes.

Evidence Official and high-trust source atlas
Inference Argument maps and scenario briefs
Design Governance memos and program framing
Speculation Low-trust lane explicitly separated
First Outputs

What ships first from the observatory.

Program docs

Project charter and source atlas

Establish the top-level research object and the input architecture behind it.

Argument work

Argument maps and subprogram framing

Translate abstract questions into inspectable positions and working subprojects.

Scenario work

Scenario briefs and governance memos

Push the research into strategic implications rather than leaving it at broad principles.

Later

A dedicated public research surface

The dossier can grow into a true research publication layer when the corpus gets larger.

Noesis in one line

If AGI becomes a durable civilizational force, we need better frameworks for co-agency than either naive replacement or naive control.

Noesis is Duckermind's long-range AGI symbiosis observatory.
