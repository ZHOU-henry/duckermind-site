Portfolio Map

Five programs, three horizons, one company language.

Duckermind exists to hold together things that usually live in separate institutions: a market-facing AI product, a serious AGI research program, a frontier signal engine, a physical AI framework, and a long-horizon embodied systems line.

Commercial edge Research umbrella Signal intelligence Embodied systems
Portfolio Principle

Do not flatten very different programs into one vague umbrella.

Parent job Give the portfolio a public structure, not a generic brand page.
Child job Let each project keep its own mission, cadence, and upgrade path.
Execution job Make every surface expandable into a real product or research portal later.
Program Matrix

What each program is actually for.

The value of the portfolio depends on role clarity. Each program exists because it handles a different operating object, produces different outputs, and matures on a different timeline.

Agora

Commercial system of record

Owns demand, supply, engagement, and delivery workflows for the agent economy.

  • Closest to revenue and operations
  • Most productized experience
  • Primary route into real market behavior
Noesis

Research umbrella

Holds the largest conceptual questions around AGI symbiosis, institutions, subjectivity, limits, and machine economy.

  • Highest research altitude
  • Explicit methodology and source discipline
  • Long-term thesis foundation for the company
Peras

Judgment and forecasting engine

Converts weakly structured frontier motion into ranked theses for builders, founders, and investors.

  • Connects research to commercial signal
  • Produces repeatable ranking outputs
  • Can become a standalone intelligence product
Physis

Engineering spine for physical AI

Owns the simulator-first technical base: perception, control, data, and ROS boundaries.

  • Practical framework lane
  • Technical staging ground for robotics work
  • Supports future embodiment programs
Titan

Long-horizon embodied research

Explores embodied intelligence as a multi-layer systems and safety problem, not a narrow robotics demo.

  • Hardware, world models, decision, safety
  • Future-oriented research lane
  • Pairs with Physis without duplicating it
Duckermind

Parent-level architecture

Shapes identity, domain structure, information hierarchy, and cross-program narrative for the full network.

  • Brand system, not product substitution
  • Creates coherence across different horizons
  • Lets every child surface keep its own identity
Why This Mix Works

The portfolio forms a loop between thought, market, and machinery.

Agora turns agent demand into observable commercial data. Noesis studies the larger social and cognitive consequences. Peras ranks where frontier value is concentrating. Physis and Titan extend the same ambition into the physical world.

Feedback loop Product surfaces generate reality pressure.
Research loop Research clarifies what the company should build next.
Signal loop Judgment products convert noise into strategic timing.
Embodiment loop Framework and safety work prevent a software-only worldview.
Domain Ladder

One clean address system now, flexible cutover paths later.

The domain model is intentionally simple. The root site acts as the portfolio atlas. Each child program gets a stable public hostname immediately. When any program needs a dedicated app, docs stack, or standalone product, its host can be promoted without changing the public brand map.

Root atlas duckermind.com

Company homepage, portfolio structure, operating model, and project dossiers.

Standalone today agora.duckermind.com

Already staged as its own premium static product surface and can later become a live application host.

Stable project hosts noesis / peras / physis / titan

Currently route into strong dossier pages. Later they can switch to reverse proxies or dedicated static roots.
