Portfolio Map

Five programs, three horizons, one company language.

Portfolio Principle

Keep the company coherent without making every program look the same.

Program Matrix

What each program is actually for.

Each program owns a different operating object and matures on a different clock.

Agora

Commercial system of record

Owns demand, supply, engagement, and delivery.

  • Closest to revenue and operations
  • Most productized experience
  • Primary route into real market behavior
Noesis

Research umbrella

Owns the biggest questions around AGI, institutions, and limits.

  • Highest research altitude
  • Explicit methodology and source discipline
  • Long-term thesis foundation for the company
Peras

Judgment and forecasting engine

Converts frontier motion into ranked theses.

  • Connects research to commercial signal
  • Produces repeatable ranking outputs
  • Can become a standalone intelligence product
Physis

Engineering spine for physical AI

Owns the simulator-first technical base.

  • Practical framework lane
  • Technical staging ground for robotics work
  • Supports future embodiment programs
Titan

Long-horizon embodied research

Explores embodied intelligence as a systems and safety problem.

  • Hardware, world models, decision, safety
  • Future-oriented research lane
  • Pairs with Physis without duplicating it
Duckermind

Parent-level architecture

Shapes identity, domains, and cross-program narrative.

  • Brand system, not product substitution
  • Creates coherence across different horizons
  • Lets every child surface keep its own identity
Why This Mix Works

The portfolio forms a loop between thought, market, and machinery.

Product creates pressure. Research creates depth. Signal sharpens timing. Embodied work expands the horizon.

Feedback loop Product surfaces generate reality pressure.
Research loop Research clarifies what the company should build next.
Signal loop Judgment products convert noise into strategic timing.
Embodiment loop Framework and safety work prevent a software-only worldview.
