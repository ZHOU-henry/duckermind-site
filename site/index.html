Design Logic

A premium AI company should not look like a generic SaaS dashboard.

The redesign borrows the best structural habits from top AI companies and research labs: strong hierarchy, clean sectional rhythm, visible systems thinking, and enough visual ambition to feel future-facing without collapsing into sci-fi noise.

Three Horizons

Near-term products, mid-term intelligence, long-term machine systems.

The portfolio works because each program sits on a different horizon. Agora is the sharpest product edge. Noesis and Peras create research and judgment leverage. Physis and Titan hold the physical and embodied trajectory.
