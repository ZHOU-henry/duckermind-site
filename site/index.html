Domain Strategy

Launch simple now, preserve freedom later.

Subdomains should exist immediately, but they do not all need full standalone applications on day one.

Phase 1

Root site + five redirecting subdomains

`duckermind.com` serves the polished portfolio site. Each project subdomain redirects to its detail page. This is the cleanest way to publish the architecture immediately.

Phase 2

Swap any subdomain to a real app when ready

For example, `agora.duckermind.com` can later stop redirecting and instead reverse proxy to the live Agora application.

Phase 3

Keep the parent site as the stable public atlas

The root site keeps linking the whole network together even when individual products diverge in stack, speed, or design.

Next Move

Ship the atlas now. Promote individual products when they are real enough to deserve their own surface.
