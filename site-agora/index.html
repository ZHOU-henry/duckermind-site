Agora

An AI agent market that behaves like a delivery system.

Agora is the most productized program in the Duckermind network: an open-source marketplace and operating layer where customer demand meets builder supply, responses become engagements, and delivery work stays visible instead of dissolving into demo theater.

Why this matters

Most “AI agent platforms” stop at catalogs. Agora models the harder middle: demand publication, provider response, acceptance, delivery, confirmation, and operations.

Product Surface

Four layers, one operational object.

The point is not to show “agents” in isolation. It is to show how demand, providers, engagements, and runs behave as one commercial and delivery system.

Demand

Publish real customer needs

Customer requests become visible operating objects instead of private forms trapped in a back office.

Supply

Let builders respond with a real commercial posture

Providers respond with timing, approach, and offer logic rather than static profile pages.

Delivery

Accepted work becomes an engagement workspace

Once accepted, the response evolves into milestones, deliverables, feedback, incident handling, and proof of work.

Ops

Operations stay visible across the lifecycle

Review queues, runs, operating metrics, and builder activity all stay legible on one system surface.
